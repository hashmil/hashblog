  - _template: content
    body: "It was super fun to play around and experiment with interesting ideas and then execute it as a few prototypes. We even did a small scale user testing. There was a lot of excitement around the project and it had a lot of promise. We even tried to sell it to a huge client in the agency. But in the end, it ended up in the ideas graveyard—where it probably truly belongs."

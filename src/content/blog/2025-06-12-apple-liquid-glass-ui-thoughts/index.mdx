When a [light-coloured notification appears over a light wallpaper](https://x.com/XorDev/status/1932806621161300225), the text becomes hard to read. The system isn't always switching the text to a darker colour to create enough contrast. That makes it hard to use day-to-day. Function should always come before looks."

  - _template: content

Everyone said the new icons were ugly and the super thin fonts were impossible to read. It was a massive change, and people complained loudly. This feels exactly the same."

  - _template: content
